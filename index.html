How to play?
  1. Click on the "Random grid" button to generate a new bingo grid
  2. Click on the check button inside a box to mark it as checked, this will turn the box red
  3. If you want to delete the text of a box, click on the delete button at the center of the respective box
  4. If you want to edit a box, click on the edit button on the right inside the respective box
  5. If you want to block the grid, click on the "Block grid" button
  6. To create your own box, click on the "Custom grid" section and fill in the details
  7. Enjoy playing FORMULA B1NGO!
